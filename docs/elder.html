"The Elderhostel Mindset


Elderhostelers might be seen
Climbing trees.
Alarming!

I've heard it said, quite aptly put,
in order that we keep
in touch

With Sprigs that, shape us,
Oh, so much,

To satisfy our learning...

Doesn't this sound charming!"
