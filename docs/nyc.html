My Great Tree! NYC


New York, NY USA

Welcome to My Great Trees! NYC,the newest section of A World Community of Old Trees,an ecology art project on the Web since 1996. Artists and students of all ages are invited to locate, identify and document their greatest NYC tree with the medium of their choice and send their work to me as an e-mail attachment. Please open the Table of Contents to see trees that have come in from all over the world. Hope you will join us!
