I have walked past my tree for five years and just two weeks ago I really noticed it. I was attracted to the tree by its curly thick branches. My tree is "treemendous"!

When I stand next to my tree, I feel like a tiny ant. The tree is like a giant mushroom when I look up. The tree has rough bark. There are not a lot of skinny branches on my tree. My tree is an oak tree that used to have two raccoons living in it.

The tree I picked is about 300 years old. It is firmly planted in the ground. If I was a squirrel, the tree would be an apartment. Its leaves protect me from rain.

My tree is elaborate.

-David
