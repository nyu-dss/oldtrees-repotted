I picked this oak tree because it is different from all of the other trees. There are two trunks on it.

My brother, my sister, my friends and I like to play tag around it in the summer. It is very big and beautiful.

When I stand next to it, I feel like I am a tiny ant looking up an enormous plant. It makes me feel small. I have to look all the way up just to see all of the branches and leaves.

-Julia
